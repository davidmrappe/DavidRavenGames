Our Games

Alienate

Whom do you trust? Betray your friends and seek allies in this fast-paced card game of secret roles and Sci-Fi tech. Play cards for other players: give them a truth serum to find out who they really are, or hand them a laser cannon to destroy your hidden enemies... you just better hope they don't use it on you! This is a free for all game of secrets and fun surprises.

Wizard's Tower

Climb the Wizard's Tower! Three intrepid adventurers band together to open seals, loot chests, and defeat the wizard's myriad of randomly generated monsters. But watch out: the wizard is cunning, a master of spells, traps, and curses. Be careful when you venture into the wizard's tower... few escape to tell the tale. This is a 3v1, asymmetrical dungeon crawler with 4 unique classes and an almost infinite combination of loot, monsters, and tower layouts.

Crew

Work together with your pirate crew in this cooperative game. Man the sails, batten down the hatches, and slay the mighty Kraken on your treacherous journey to treasure island. But beware... only one pirate can assemble the treasure map and claim the treasure! This is a cooperative game where up to six players battle against a deck of hazards while competing against each other for pieces of a coveted treasure map.

Draw!

It's high noon. The saloon doors swing in the wind, dust devils drift down a sun-baked road, and the cowboys and cowgirls of this boom town gather for a good old fashioned shootout! Draw your cards, draw your six-shooters, and point your finger guns at your friends to shoot them down. Are you the fastest gun in the west, or just another dinner for the circling vultures? This is a fast paced, free for all card game for 3 to 10 players.

About Us

David Raven Games is a board game company run by Portland game designer David Rappe. David has been designing board games since he was six years old, and has been playing them as long as he can remember.

He likes thinking of new game mechanics that haven't been done before, always trying to make his games thought-provoking and easy to learn. David loves games that have clever, clean mechanics without a lot of fluff, and although he enjoys complexity, he doesn't like unnecessary complication. Above all else, David's golden rule when designing games is simple: everyone should be having fun at all times!

David gets a lot of help from his friends and loved ones, who are always willing to test his games, pitch in with great ideas, and critique his less-inventive ones.

David's favorite board games are Game of Thrones: The Board Game, War of the Ring, and Coup. He plays the Middle Earth Strategy Battle Game, Magic: The Gathering, Sorcery: Contested Realm, and the Middle Earth Collectible Card Game.

Join David on his vlog, and if you're interested in any of his games, sign up for his mailing list and/or support him on Kickstarter. If you're interested in providing graphic design work, artwork, or you want to collaborate on a project, please feel free to reach out to him here.
